Hello, Love, Again

This film is a sequel from the movie "Hello, Love, Goodbye" that talks about two lovers. After fighting for their love and chance of survival against different timezone and distance, the main characters meet once again in Canada but realize that things weren't the same as they was before in Hongkong.

When Joy leaves HongKong for Canada, she starts her new life with her second name, Marie. However, even if Joy wanted to move on and forget the past, that's where Ethan will show up. After many years of managing a successful bar business, Ethan comes to his downfall. He then goes to Canada to look for opportunities of work and rise once again. That is where they meet and through hardships and doubts with one another, they Joy began to realize that Ethan has changed and accept him again.
