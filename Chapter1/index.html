Sizzling Chicken Sisig

Sizzling chicken sisig is a popular dish in the Philippines. The use of chicken is considered #p is used to enter additional details regarding the title written in h1 healthier compared to other types of sisig dishes such as pork. This type of dish has many ways to cook and this is how I want my Sizzling chicken sisig to be served.

Ingredients

  • Grilled Chicken Breast - Chopped
  • Chicken Liver - Chopped (Optional)
  • White Onion - Minced
  • Garlic Powder
  • Serrano pepper (Diagonally Sclied)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking Oil
  • Green Chili

    • Procedures

      1. Heat the cooking oil in a pan.
      2. Saute the onions until softened.
      3. Add the chicken liver and cook until brown.
      4. Add the chicken breast. Stir for 3-4 minutes.
      5. Put the garlic powder and soy sauce. Stir.
      6. Sprinkle salt and pepper. Stir.
      7. Add the mayonnaise. Stir.
      9. Heat the sizzling plate.
      10. Once the pan is hot, add the butter and melt.
      11. Transfer the chicken sisig on the heated pan.
      12. Crack the egg and drop in the middle of the chicken.
      13. Add the green chili.
      14. Serve and Enjoy!
